Our Story

In 2023, STEAK opened the Taste of Meato Restaurant in his Hometown near Country Village and welcomed our first customer through the doors. In the following years, we'd held countless celebrations with families and a lot of other people, from birthdays, graduations, engagements, anniversaries, career milestones even retirement dinners.

Conventionally, having steak at all was always considered a luxury, a special occasion, a once in a year thing, if at all, and was often hit or miss too. Thus our goal was and still, is to make remarkable steaks available to everyone.

Since 2023

After our months of beef research (yes it's actually a thing) we finally got the hang of how to butcher properly on our own and discovered that those secondary cuts from the very best beef could be incredibly tasty as well and is actually affordable, you never know how great steaks can be.

With a lot of twists and turns and help from a huge community of committed people working together to achieve the same goal. We finally made it people.

It’s where we started, where we are, and where we deeply hope to stay.
